How to play

The aim of the game is to guess the footballer correctly. You will be given a list of players that this footballer has played with.

You only need to enter the footballers last name. Everytime you give an incorrect answer, another player will be added to the list.

Guess the footballer in as few attempts as possible and share your score with your friends!
